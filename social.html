Popular Topic: what's an Ondes Martenot?
Created by: KillerMethBaby1996
Electronic music stems back decades ago. An early example of on of these instruments is the Ondes Martenot. The instrument lets out an eerie wavering of a note. Sounds are produced by the frequency of oscillation in vacuum tubes inside the instrument. It's design is similar to a keyboard. The ondes martenot had largely fell into obscurity until recently.
