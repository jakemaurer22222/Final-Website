Lessons: We'll teach anything


For years upon years, we have been teaching student of all ages and instruments how to perform. Our highly skilled teachers can teach you anything from an ondes martenot to a glockenspiel. Don't know anything about music? No problem, you'll learn. Know everything about music? Good for you. As you can see to right, we even teach toddlers. Our rigorous and controversial teaching methods are designed to make even the most musically impaired masters at the craft. Each student also has the chance after at least one year of training to partake in our annual in-store recitals. Test your skills against other students in a rigorous battle of sounds. The grand prize is glory and fame while the lowest ranking competitors shalled be imposed into exile. Lesson plans start off at $499.99 per month with one 6-hour lesson everyday for one year. After the year has passed, students are brought before a council where they're skills are then evaluated on whether or not they are to be passed onto the next stage of they're training.To the left here is the leader of our school of music. He wears the same sandals everyday and is efficient at all instruments known to the world. He has trained students for 900 years and is only known as master to those near him. Otherwise, his name is unknown. He has no phone number, no social media, no trackable address, and is only seen during his time at the school. Some say that Robert Johnson had sold his soul to by a creek down in Mississippi in exchange for mastery of his instrument. Master rarely speaks except to students of the school when brought upon the council for trials.
